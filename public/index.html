Our Story

Born from Love,
Built for Peace

Talibon Memorial Park was established with a single, heartfelt purpose: to give the people of Talibon and the wider Bohol community a dignified and beautiful place to lay their loved ones to rest.

Our founders, deeply moved by the need for a serene and professionally managed memorial space in the region, dedicated themselves to creating a park that families could trust — not only as a resting place but as a living sanctuary of memory, faith, and love.

Over the years, we have had the privilege of serving thousands of families during their most difficult moments. Through compassion, commitment, and care, Talibon Memorial Park has become more than a resting place — it is a place where memories are preserved and legacies are honored.

Today, our park continues to grow — not just in size, but in the depth of care we provide to every family who entrusts us with the memory of their loved ones.

Our Mission

To provide a peaceful, dignified, and beautifully maintained memorial space where families can honor their loved ones with grace — now and for generations to come.

We are committed to delivering compassionate service, maintaining the highest standards of park care, and supporting families through every step of their memorial journey.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted, respected, and serene memorial park in the Bohol region — a sanctuary where the lives of those who have passed are honored with dignity, beauty, and lasting peace.

We envision a park that is not only a place of rest but a living garden of memory — a space that brings comfort, healing, and hope to all who visit.
